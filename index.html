Didier Chételat

  • Research Scientist, Polytechnique Montréal
  • didier.chetelat@polymtl.ca
    Google Scholar
I am a machine learning research scientist at the Canada Excellence Research Chair in Data Science for Real Time Decision Making, under the leadership of Andrea Lodi. I obtained my Ph.D. in Statistics at Cornell University in 2015 under the supervision of Martin T. Wells.
My main research interests center around deep learning for combinatorial optimization. I also work on applied projects, most recently in finance, transportation, medical imaging and physical engineering.
